Early Life & Biography

Paul Sorvino, whose full name is Paul Anthony Sorvino, was born on the 13th of April 1939 in Bensonhurst, Brooklyn, New York, the U.S. to parents, Angela Maria Mattea and Ford Sorvino.  His mother was a housemaker and piano teacher and was of Italian descent. His father was an Italian immigrant who worked as a foreman in a robe factory. When it comes to his education, he has attended Lafayette High School and the American Musical and Dramatic Academy.
