Who Was Ralph Emery Wife?

Ralph Emery leaves behind his three sons, five grandkids, and seven great-grandchildren, as well as his lovely wife, Joy Emery.

Following that, Ralph married Betty Fillmore Emery Etheridge, but this also did not take long to end as the pair divorced soon after.

Finally, Ralph met the love of his life, Joy, with whom he would spend the rest of his life. Joy and Ralph had tied the knot in 1967.
