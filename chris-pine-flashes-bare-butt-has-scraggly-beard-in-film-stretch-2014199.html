Chris Pine Flashes Bare Butt, Has Scraggly Beard in Film Stretch

Ow ow! Chris Pine flashes his bare butt in upcoming comedy Stretch and Us Weekly has an exclusive first look at his derriere.

Sporting that perfect tush, Pine plays billionaire entrepreneur Karos in the upcoming flick, which revolves around a limo driver (Patrick Wilson) who has fallen into a slump after being dumped by his girlfriend (Brooklyn Decker). Wilson’s character, who is in dire need of cash, is then left with Pine’s character as his client for the evening. Karos promises to cover his debt if he meets all of his outrageous requests during a night that, naturally, goes utterly wrong. Helping Pine’s character throughout the flick is Jessica Alba, who plays his dispatcher Charlie.

In the clip, the hunk looks almost unrecognizable with a bushy beard, pilot’s glasses and headgear, wearing just a tiger backpack and a parachuting device as he slow-twerks. Wilson asks a disheveled Pine: “Are you okay?”
