What Happened Between Katy Perry And Lady Gaga?

Katy Perry and Lady Gaga achieved fame around the same time, and they practically grew up in the music industry together. These days, fans are swooning over their friendship goals activities.The two music icons seem to be chummy with each other on numerous occasions. It’s also pretty obvious that the two have mutual respect for each other as they never negatively talked about one another during interviews.In fact, they both have nothing but good words when talking about each other. Despite the seemingly good relationship between them in the public eyes today, the two are actually pitted against each other for years in throughout the 2010s. So what really happened between the two? Is there a basis for their alleged feud that the fans have suspected for years?

The Constant Denial Of The Feud Between Lady Gaga And Katy Petty

Through the years, both singers have constantly denied that they have a feud. In one of Lady Gaga’s interviews, she said that she understands why some fans are pitting them against each other. She stated that just because their singles are released within a week of each other does not mean that they have a beef.

Although Lady Gaga thinks that releasing albums at the same time is not convenient for all the fans and might create a little awkwardness between them, there are no negative feelings between them. She further added that she likes Katy and her fans.

Katy Perry also denied the allegations that she had a feud with Lady Gaga. Rumors of their feud resurfaced after Katy Perry tweeted about using blasphemy as a form of entertainment.

At the time, Lady Gaga’s Alejandro was just released and people assumed that Katy was talking about the song. Katy who was raised as a Christian denied that the tweet was about Gaga’s song, she also added that her tweet does not target anything specific and just a general thing.

What Exactly Happened Between Katy Perry And Lady Gaga?

Lady Gaga and Katy Perry are always pitted against each other due to the fact that they both debuted in 2008. Comparison between the two throughout their careers is inevitable since they started in the music industry at the same time. The fans of both singers constantly stirred the pots with their comparison, which might have resulted to drive a wedge between the two.

It also did not help that throughout their music careers, their music seems to be always competing against each other. Katy’s single Roar and Lady Gaga’s single Applause were released just days apart but because of a leak, both singles debuted on the same day.

Some years down the line, the feud started to gain public attention once again after Katy was dragged to the legal battle between Dr. Luke and Kesha. She even had to testify on the court during the trial since claims of Dr. Luke raping her came to light, but she denied the said allegations.

Amidst the trial, it was revealed that Kesha and Lady Gaga have been talking about Katy through texts. The texts show that Kesha told Lady Gaga that Katy has also been raped by Dr. Luke. Kesha expressed to Lady Gaga that she’s upset with Katy, the singer then asked Kesha if she wanted her to talk to Katy in Kesha’s behalf. Lady Gaga then called the Roar singer mean.

How The Alleged Feud Between Katy Perry And Lady Gaga Ended

The alleged feud ended after Lady Gaga finally addressed the issue immediately after the release of the texts. The Poker Face singer tweeted that since they grew up in the entertainment industry together, they both gone through celebrations and differences together.

In the tweet that was dated November 30, 2018, Lady Gaga acknowledged that the texts revealed in the court was legit and said that they were old texts. The Born This Way singer further added that both of them have matured since and already gotten over the incident.

Lady Gaga expressed her love for Katy and called the Fireworks singer her friend with a kind soul. The said tweet was not left unnoticed by Katy since she replied to Lady Gaga’s tweet.

The Hot N’ Cold singer who has topped the Billboard charts numerous times has called Lady Gaga her friend and even said that she loves her. The exchange of tweets between the two established that they are friendly and have nothing but respect for each other.

There seems to be no bad blood between the two singers as they were seen snapping some pictures together since. The two singers were seen together in a photo on the weekend of the highly anticipated Academy Museum. Both singers attended the star-studded event, and it’s safe to say that they interacted with each other behind the scenes.
