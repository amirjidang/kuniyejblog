Aubrey Edwards Makes Karen Jarrett Tap Out To Figure-Four On 6/16 AEW Rampage

Aubrey Edwards and Papa Briscoe are undefeated in AEW.

On the June 16 episode of AEW Rampage, Edwards teamed up with Mark and Papa Briscoe to face Jeff and Karen Jarrett and Jay Lethal. The match marked a new high in the feud between Edwards and the Jarretts, which spans back to January. The rivalry picked up speed when Karen smashed Aubrey with a guitar at AEW Double or Nothing, but Aubrey got her revenge on Friday.

In a chaotic mixed person trios match, the fan-favorite referee got the last laugh, as she hit Lethal with a guitar and ultimately scored the victory by making Karen tap out to the Figure-Four leg-lock.
