Who Is Jake Lamb?

Jake Lamb is a baseball infielder for the Seattle Mariners of Major League Baseball (MLB).

He launched his MLB career with the Arizona Diamondbacks in 2014, and became an All-Star in 2017.

Aside from the Arizona Diamondbacks, Jake has also played for the Oakland Athletics, Chicago White Sox, Toronto Blue Jays and Los Angeles Dodgers.

Jake Lamb Early Life

Jake grew up in Seattle, Washington and was schooled in Bishop Blanchet High School, also in Seattle.

He played baseball in high school and upon graduation, got selected by the Pittsburgh Pirates in the 38th round of the 2009 MLB Draft.

He proceeded to attend college at the University of Washington, where he also played baseball for the Washington Huskies.

Jake won the All-Pac-12 Conference player.

During summer, Jake would play played for the Yarmouth–Dennis Red Sox of the Cape Cod Baseball League.
