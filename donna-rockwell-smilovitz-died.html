Dr. Donna Rockwell Smilovitz, a Renowned Clinical Psychologist; Died at 66

It is heartbreaking news that a passionate Renowned Psychologist, Dr.Donna, has passed away unexpectedly. Dr.Donna, a licensed clinical psychologist with private practices in New York and Suburban Detroit, passed away at age 66 on October 7, 2023.

The family of Dr.Donna Rockwell Smilovitz is in mourning cause their beloved part of the family has gone too far from this world; we hope wherever she is, her soul will remain in peace.

Who was Dr. Donna Rockwell Smilovitz?

Dr. Rockwell completed a Bachelor of Arts in Language and Communication from Hampshire College in Amherst, Massachusetts. She graduated from American University in Washington, D.C., with a master’s in journalism and public affairs. Dr. Rockwell received her Master’s and Doctorate in Humanistic and Clinical Psychology from Farmington Hills, Michigan’s Centre for Humanistic Studies, now known as the Michigan School of Psychology.

Dr. Donna’s husband was Bernie Smilovitz, and despite being a grandma, mother, and wife, she never lost her passion for what she did.

She was the founder and CEO of the online community and program Already Famous: Embracing Inner Celebrity, encouraging women and girls to live their best, most genuine lives. She was an acknowledged student of mindfulness meditation, an expert in celebrity mental health, and a proponent of modern humanistic psychology.

Dr. Donna, a mental health specialist with 20 years of expertise, has been featured on TV, radio, and social media. Among other publications, The New York Times, The Washington Post, Newsweek, The Wall Street Journal, The Atlantic, The BBC, and The CBC have all highlighted her studies on the psychology of fame and stardom.
