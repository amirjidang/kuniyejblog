The Truth About Emma Roberts' Relationship With Her Mother

Emma Roberts, a beautiful film performer and singer, was welcomed in a closely connected family to the moviemaking industry. The talented actress has always been inspired by her worldwide known aunt Julia Roberts, and that's why she decided to pursue an acting career.

Her professional path began with a debut role in a movie called Blow in 2001. After that, she attended an art school in Los Angeles to gain more knowledge, practice, and experience. She rose to stardom, having starred in the super popular television series Unfabulous since 2004 up till 2007. Then she appeared in several successful TV projects, including American Horror Story: Apocalypse, Scream Queens, Billionaire Boys Club, Paradise Hills, and many others.

In addition, the celebrity can boast excellent vocal skills. On top of that, Emma succeeds not only professionally but also in her personal life. Let's see who surrounds such a gifted Hollywood star!

Emma Roberts' Family

The father's name is Eric Anthony Roberts, and it wouldn't be surprising to say that he is also an actor who was nominated for the Golden Globe Award several times. Despite this, he also had "dark" days when he was arrested a few times for domestic violence and drugs. Perhaps, that's why Emma and her dad issues in their relationship. However, they are back to normal now.

The mother, Kelly Cunningham, is also a part of the filmmaking business. Her marriage to Eric was relatively short-lived, and she soon walked down the aisle for the second time with the musician Kelly Nickels. The mother and daughter share a tight bond.

The family also includes famous aunt Julia Roberts, who became a renowned star thanks to her leading role in Pretty Woman of 1990. It was she who influenced Emma's choice of becoming a movie star.

The celebrity also has a maternal half-sister, Grace, who was born in 2001. She deals with art, and her talent can be seen in Emma's social media account. The sisters are very close.

As for Emma's personal life, since 2019 and up till the present moment, Emma has been romantically involved with film performer Garrett Hedlund. These two lovebirds started as friends, but their feeling quickly grew into a love story.

Instagram War With Her Mom

The actress opened up about her Instagram war with her mother that she never saw coming and what happened when her mom spilled the tea about her pregnancy.

Fans of Emma Roberts know that back in August 2020, she revealed that she and Garrett were expecting their first child together. Ever since then, Emma has shared everything about her life as a mom with her over 16 million followers on Instagram.

In a new interview with Jimmy Kimmel, Emma revealed that her pregnancy announcement didn't exactly go as planned. Emma explained that her mom Kelly Cunningham accidentally spilled the tea about her daughter's pregnancy thanks to her new iPhone and Instagram account.

She revealed, "I was keeping my pregnancy pretty low key, but unfortunately, my mother has Instagram. Mothers and Instagram... it's a bad combination."

Emma explained that up until recently, her mom has never had a computer, and she only ever used a flip phone to communicate. She decided it would be a nice idea to get her mom an iPhone for Mother's Day so they could FaceTime and iMessage to communicate more during these strange times. As soon as Emma got her mom her first iPhone, she instantly regretted it.

Emma Roberts' Mother Ruining a Special Moment for Her

The actress said her mom figured out how to set up a Google alert about her, and then all of a sudden, Emma was being flooded with headlines, and her mom asked, "Emma is this true?!" her mom would also send old photos and be like "Emma have you seen this photo from 2011?"

But the actress said as bad as that was, things took a turn for the worse when her mom got Instagram. Her mom slowly started gaining more and more followers and even experienced getting catfished by imposter celebrity accounts.

Emma said that her mom also started messaging her fans and thanked them for their pregnancy wishes before Emma had officially announced.

Forgiving Her Mom

The Scream Queens' star said she was on a plane at the time when her mom broke the news, so she couldn't even call her or get to her to stop her from sharing the most important news of her life.

Emma decided to punish her mom in the worst way possible for a mother-daughter relationship: she blocked her.

However, Emma eventually unblocked her mom, and they can laugh about it now, with the actress saying, "It'll be a good story to tell the baby boy."

Fans were quick to react to Emma's story sharing similar experiences with their own mom. Despite the drama, Emma and her mom remain close and taking care of the baby.
