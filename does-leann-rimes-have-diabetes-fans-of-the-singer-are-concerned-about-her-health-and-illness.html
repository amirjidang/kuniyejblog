Does LeAnn Rimes Have Diabetes?

No, LeAnn Rimes does not have diabetes; however, she does have a skin condition named Psoriasis, which is prone to diabetes. The singer has opened up about her health condition with Future of Personal Health.

As mentioned by Rimes, her skin disorder is also vulnerable to other diseases like diabetes, heart disease, and arthritis. However, she does not seems to suffer from diabetes now.

Also, there is no news on an American singer LeAnn going through diabetes. Regardless, she is raising awareness of the condition as it affects the person’s mental health.
