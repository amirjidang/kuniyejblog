Jessica Biel's Business Ventures Have Not All Been Successful

Jessica Biel was accused of stealing tips from her now-defunct restaurant

While she may have found success with her other ventures, Jessica's attempt at being a restaurateur didn't go as planned. The mom of two opened her eatery in 2016 but closed it after only two years of running it. Speaking to Jimmy Kimmel, she revealed that the restaurant wasn't making money.

"We’re definitely not making money," Jessica explained. "Nobody’s making money in the restaurant business, in my experience, at least not yet."

The situation escalated when the star's ex-employees accused her and her partners of stealing $430,100 in tips.

In 2018, Radar Online reported, "Au Fudge employees accused Biel, 36, and her business partners of stealing $430,100 in tips. The employees claimed they worked over 8 hours a day without meal or rest breaks. They also claimed they didn’t receive overtime compensation."
