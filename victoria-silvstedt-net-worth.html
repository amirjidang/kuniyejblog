Karin Victoria Silvstedt has an estimated net worth of $17 million. She is a Swedish actress, singer, television personality and a celebrity model. She has modelled for a lot of people including FHM, Playboy and Channel. She has also hosted numerous award shows, released a successful dance album and appeared in various television shows. Moreover, she has acted as a star in her own Reality Series titled "My Perfect Life". She has also appeared in numerous Hollywood television series such as Melrose Place, Malibu and CA

She was born on 19th September 1974 in Skelleftehamn Sweden. She was raised in a family of five and has a younger brother and an older sister. As a baby, she wanted to be a veterinarian and was interested in horseback riding.

Victoria Silvstedt's father worked as a captain of a certain local ski team. At the age of five in the year 1989, Silvstedt emerged fourth at a youth championship which was won by Wiberg in the giant slalom. However, an accident while skiing at the age of 16 ended this career and she embarked on her present careers.
