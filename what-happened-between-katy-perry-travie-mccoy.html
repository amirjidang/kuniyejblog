What Really Happened Between Katy Perry And Travie McCoy?

Katy Perry welcomed her daughter with Orlando Bloom in 2020. She hasn't always been lucky in love but seems to have found her happily ever after. Before that though, the singer dated Gym Class Heroes frontman Travie McCoy for nearly two years. Perry even appeared in the band's music video for "Cupid's Chokehold."

However, the pair called it quits and Katy allegedly wrote a song about their relationship.

Katie is without a doubt among the most popular and celebrated pop stars of our generation. For that reason, her private life is just as talked about as her music is. Her love life always makes headlines, from her bitter divorce from Russell Brand to her relationship with Orlando Bloom. But what really happened with Travie McCoy?

Their Careers Were Just Starting To Take Off

The '00s are fondly remembered for the catchy tunes, low-rise jeans, and shiny lip gloss. That's not all; Gym Class Heroes blessed the world with their anthem "Cupid's Chokehold" in 2015. Katy, who was dating Travie at the time, played his love interest in the music video of the song. The famous exes met in 2007 and dated on and off for a year.

They met at a recording studio and hit it off. Katy was just starting out, and Gym Class Heroes were basking in fame from the success of their second studio album. They were a fan favorite pair whose fans rooted for.

The duo collaborated again when they co-headlined a tour in 2008, their careers were taking an upward trajectory. After the release of Katy's hit song, "I Kissed a Girl," her career took off.

The pair would later call it quits, and at the time, neither of them addressed the breakup nor what led to it.

Katy Wrote A Song About Their Relationship

Per Vibe, during VH1’s Behind The Music special, the rapper dished on the relationship and subsequent breakup. Ultimately, her fame and his substance abuse led to their split. It was Katy who ended things, and she did it via email!

He revealed, "She wasn't stupid… She knew when I was f***** up. I chose dr*gs over our relationship… As things started taking off for her, the more I started to doubt my role in her life. There were times I felt like a stepping ladder."

After they called it quits, Katy released "Circle the Drain", which was alleged to be about Travie and his addiction.

The rapper and singer addressed the rumors in an interview with MTV News. He wasn't too pleased with the "I Kissed a Girl" singer. McCoy stated, "I heard she put out a song that's about me, or about some old habits or whatever. [And] I look at it like this: I'm just stoked that she finally has a song with some substance on her record. Good job."
