15 Things That Never Made Sense About Ross And Chandler's Friendship

The friendship between Ross and Chandler is an interesting one. There are a lot of things that happened between them that never really made a lot of sense. Out of the six main characters on Friends, each member has a different relationship with their surrounding circle. For example, the friendship between Joey and Phoebe is much different than the friendship between Monica and Rachel. And both of those friendships are far different from the friendship that Ross and Chandler share.

Ross and Chandler have the type of friendship that has stood the test of time since they have been friends for so many years. They have also overcome plenty of difficulties between the two of them, including having crushes on the same girl and arguing over which man is stronger. They both have found themselves attracted to members of each other’s family as well! We've noticed that there are a few things about their friendship that don’t really make sense.

15 Ross Kissed A Girl That Chandler Liked In College

It doesn’t make sense to us that Ross would kiss a girl that Chandler had a crush on! There is an unspoken code that goes between friends to prevent this sort of thing. Friends who truly care about each other wouldn’t kiss someone that their friend was attracted to or had feelings for.

14 Chandler Kissed Rachel To Get Revenge On Ross

After Ross kissed a girl that Chandler liked in college, Chandler decided to get revenge by planting a kiss on Rachel! He knew that Rachel was the object of Ross’s affection which is why he decided to do what he did. Friends usually don’t try to seek revenge on each other this way.

13 Ross Moved In With Chandler (And Joey,) Totally Inconveniencing Them

At one point in time, Ross pretty much lost everything. He was financially suffering and due to his hardships, he moved in with Joey and Chandler. They were willing to help him get by. When he moved in with Joey and Chandler, he totally inconvenienced their apartment setup and their lives!

12 Chandler Suggested That Ross Make A Pros & Cons List About Rachel

Ross infamously made a "pros and cons" list about Rachel, listing the best qualities about her and the worst qualities about her. What some people might forget is that Chandler is actually the person who gave him the idea to make that list in the first place.

11 Ross Blamed His Teenage Substance Use On Chandler

In order to make himself look better in front of his parents, Ross blamed the substance use he briefly was part of in high school on Chandler! He didn’t mind if Chandler’s reputation got messed up with his parents because he wanted to stay in his parents' good graces.

10 Chandler Tried To Convince Ross To Move Into A Lame Apartment

When Chandler and Joey were tired of Ross living at their apartment, they did their best to try to convince Ross to move somewhere else. They even were willing to try to convince Ross to move into an apartment that was totally unsuitable! True friends would never do something like that.

9 Ross Accidentally Kissed Monica After A Moment of Miscommunication With Chandler

After Chandler kissed Rachel during a college party with a lot of alcohol, he left her laying on his bed. Ross missed the memo and thought he was giving Rachel smooch when really, he was accidentally giving his own sister, Monica, a kiss on the lips! When they discovered this fact many years later, they were both disgusted.

8 Chandler Wrote That Ross Had Passed Away Online As A Joke

As an online joke, Chandler thought it would be funny to write that Ross had passed away. This joke goes way too far and pushes the boundaries of what is acceptable and what isn’t. Joking about someone passing away is a little bit too serious. This is not something that Chandler should have joked about on the Internet.

7 Ross Was Angry & Disgusted About Chandler And Monica's Relationship At First

When Ross first saw Chandler and Monica hooking up through the window, he was infuriated. He started yelling in anger! He marched over to Monica's apartment to break them up. After they explained that they loved each other, he softened up completely, but he was angry at first!

6 Chandler Was Unable To Stop Staring At Ross's Cousin Cassie

When Ross and Monica's cousin, Cassie, arrived to spend some time, Chandler was unable to keep his eyes off of her! He had a major crush on her. Ross is the one who actually ended up making the situation weird because he also found himself attracted to his own cousin. Which is super gross.

5 Ross And Chandler Challenged Each Other To An Arm Wrestling Match Due To Their Egos

Ross and Chandler were dealing with their own egotistical problems when they decided to challenge each other to an arm-wrestling match at a Halloween costume party. They were trying to convince themselves and each other of who was the stronger man. How immature can they be?

4 Chandler (And Joey) Use Ross' Infant Son To Pick Up Women And Then Leave Him On A Bus

After Ross’ son, Ben, came into the picture, Chandler and Joey ended up taking Ben out for the day in New York City to help them try to pick up women. During this time, Chandler and Joey accidentally left baby Ben on the bus! They were way too distracted to be babysitting.

3 Chandler Didn't Speak Up To Advise Ross Against Marrying Emily

As Ross’ best friend, Chandler should’ve spoken up about Ross‘ wedding to Emily. He should have offered some advice and insight on the fact that Ross wasn’t making the best decision by marrying Emily. Chandler should’ve spoken up... but the rest of the group should have too.

2 Ross Kissed Chandler's Mother

At one point, Ross actually kissed Chandler's mother! Talk about inappropriate! It isn’t cool to make out with your best friend's mother, but for some reason Ross didn’t seem to care. He did exactly what he felt like doing at the moment without thinking about Chandler‘s feelings.

1 Ross And Chandler Kissed Each Other

During season three of the show, Chandler planted a kiss on Ross's mouth when he was drunk. It's weird that these two best friends ended up kissing each other. But... the fact that most members of the cast have kissed each other at one point or another makes this much less of a big deal.
