Milton Jones of Norfolk, Virginia, Died in A Tragic Crash

Milton Jones: A fatal motorcycle accident claimed a man’s life in Virginia Beach, Virginia.

Milton Jones of Norfolk, Virginia, passed away in a tragic crash. The details of his untimely demise have not yet been shared with the public; however, unconfirmed reports say that Jones got into a motorcycle accident that left him fatally injured on Saturday, September 2, 2023, that left his family and loved ones devastated.

He was a painter. He went to Salem High School. He lived in Norfolk, Virginia Beach, Virginia.

An obituary was released declaring his sudden departure from the world. A request was also made to allow the family the time to make appropriate arrangements and respect their mourning.

Friends and loved ones have been offering their deepest condolences.

Ashley Abbott, moody of Virginia and Jones, knew each other since childhood. She wrote a heartfelt note on social media: “You are truly the definition of loyal. If I had known when I saw you a few months ago that it would have been my last time seeing you, I would have hugged you longer.”

“I just can’t believe you’re gone Milton Jones. You were one of the coolest, you always made sure me and the girls were good whenever we came around! Y’all really don’t play about us! You will be truly missed with your silly tail self”, another wrote.

Our condolences to the family and the loved ones. May his soul rest in peace.
