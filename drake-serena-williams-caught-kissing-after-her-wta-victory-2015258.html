Drake, Serena Williams Caught Kissing After Her WTA Victory

Looks like things between Drake and Serena Williams have gone from 0 to 100. The rapper, 28, and the tennis star, 33, were caught on camera getting cozy while out to dinner in Cincinnati, Ohio, on Sunday, Aug. 23 — and let’s just say they seem more than friendly.

Pictures posted by TMZ on Monday appear to show the Wimbledon champ and the Degrassi alum kissing in a back room at upscale Italian eatery Sotto. According to the site, the pair hit up the downtown hot spot after Williams won the Women’s Tennis Association tournament.

Rumors about the two have been making the rounds for months. Earlier this summer, in July, Drake was spotted cheering on the athlete at Wimbledon, where she battled her way to a fourth straight majors victory.

After one match — during which Williams triumphed over Maria Sharapova — a reporter asked whether the “Started From the Bottom” hitmaker might be her “lucky mascot.” According to the Associated Press, Drake, who was at the press conference, laughed and covered his face.

“We’ve been friends for, like, so many years,” Williams replied (via the AP). “Just like family.”

Family or not, Drake has made his admiration of the superstar tennis pro very clear. While at Wimbledon, he posted a photo of Williams and her sister Venus, captioning it, “A story that will never be duplicated.”
