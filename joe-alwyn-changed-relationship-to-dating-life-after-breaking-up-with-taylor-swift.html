Joe Alwyn Changed His Relationship To His Dating Life After Breaking Up With Taylor Swift

Highlights

  • Joe Alwyn keeps his dating life private due to the intrusive and increasingly intrusive nature of our culture.
  • Alwyn's relationship with Taylor Swift was strained due to the lack of privacy and constant media attention.
  • Alwyn's preference for privacy extends to his friendships and personal life, as he prefers to focus on his work as an actor.

Taylor Swift has made headlines for her catchy music as much as for her romantic life; although the two are inexplicably linked as the songstress has often written pieces about her relationships. It's no wonder, as Swift has dated some of the biggest heartthrobs in Hollywood, including Joe Jonas, Harry Styles, John Mayer, Jake Gyllenhaal, and Joe Alwyn.

Swift and Alwyn dated for a considerable time before they made the shocking move of calling it quits. While neither star addressed the media regarding the cause of their split, rumors swirled that Joe Alwyn left Taylor Swift to date actress Emma Laird. These allegations were never confirmed nor denied, but the British-born actor has taken pains to keep his romantic life private for several surprising reasons.

Why Joe Alwyn Has Kept His Dating Life Private Since His Breakup With Taylor Swift

For celebrities and other public figures, navigating the dating scene comes with many challenges, given the intense scrutiny and spotlight they are under. For starters, it is hard for many to maintain a semblance of privacy and form a genuine bond. In addition, the added pressure to control how they are viewed by the public and portrayed in the media often strains even the most loving relationships.

The best example is the relationship between Taylor Swift and Joe Alwyn. While the couple lasted longer than many other Hollywood romances (they were together for six years), it's no secret that the lack of privacy and constant media attention caused a strain in their relationship.

As reported by Vanity Fair, The Brutalist actor spoke to Elle UK in April 2022 about several things, including why it was important to him to keep his romance with Taylor Swift low-key.

The actor stated that he couldn't fathom why keeping his love life private would be seen as an unusual thing to do. He added:

It's not really because I want to be guarded and private; it's more a response to something else. We live in a culture that is so increasingly intrusive. The more you give–and frankly, even if you don't give it–something will be taken.

Despite Joe Alwyn's attempts to keep his relationship with Taylor Swift private, the singer's too famous to keep her love life secret, a fact many fans have since said Alwyn should have realized when he first started dating her in 2016.

As many know, Swift has since moved on and dated British rocker Matt Haley and, more recently, football player Travis Kelce. But who has Joe Alwyn dated?

Given the actor's penchant for secrecy in his relationships, it's no surprise that not much information is out there on who he may or may not have dated since his breakup with Taylor Swift.

There were rumors that shortly after dating Swift (and even allegedly even while he was with her), Alwyn quickly moved on with actress Emma Laird.

Laird and Alwyn met while filming the crime drama The Brutalist, which tells the story of a Jewish architect who outlived the atrocities of the Holocaust. Filming for the project was ongoing in April 2023, just as reports surfaced that Joe Alwyn and Taylor Swift had broken up.

The first piece of evidence that hinted the two actors might be dating was a picture Emma Laird posted of Joe Alwyn on her Instagram, with a caption that read: 'Moments in March," followed by a red heart emoji. The second was a person close to Laird who told The Daily Mail: "Emma has been there for Joe since news of his split from Taylor emerged."

What Joe Alwyn's Friends Have Said About Why The Actor Has Remained Private About His Love Life

While many fans held out hope for a time that Taylor Swift and Joe Alwyn would rekindle their romance, it was not to be. And despite the intense public scrutiny and attention over their six-year relationship, it appears that wasn't the only cause of their split.

According to People Magazine, one insider source stated that one of the reasons behind their break-up was the COVID-19 pandemic, "They had plenty in common and fell in love in a safe bubble while she was retreating from the world during Reputation," the anonymous source said.

Then the pandemic hit, and they were locked down together and able to continue growing their relationship in this insulated way. But he didn't really 'know' her yet outside of that bubble.

Joe Alwyn's friends have also gone on record to discuss why Alywn prefers to remain quiet regarding his personal life and relationships. One friend told the Daily Mail in 2017 that, "Women want to throw themselves at him. It's the way he holds himself. But to a certain extent, he's always been married to his work."

The actor also revealed to British Vogue in 2018 that while he was aware that people wanted to know about his private life, he preferred to discuss his acting.
