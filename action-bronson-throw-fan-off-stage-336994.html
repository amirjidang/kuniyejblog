Action Bronson Throws Fan Off Stage In NYC

Every now and again, Action Bronson likes to toss fans from the stage and back into the crowd if they attempt to interrupt his set. His latest hurling of a human being occurred last night (Mar. 24) at New York City’s Terminal 5.

During the album release show for his Mr. Wonderful debut, Bronson’s stage was bombarded by a fan as he performed his “Amadu Diablo” freestyle with Party Supplies. Barely missing a beat, the Queens rapper grabbed the fan by the shirt and tossed him back to the crowd. It is clear that this has become a normal occurrence, as Bronson returned to his rhymes with notable ease.
