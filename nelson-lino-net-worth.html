Nelson Lino Net Worth | TheRichest

Nelson Lino

Birthname Nelson Luis Ferreira Lino Birthplace Almada, Portugal Profession Producer Net worth $2,000,000 Source of Wealth Music Production, Investments Nationality Portugal

American music executive and producer Nelson Lino has an estimated net worth of $2 million in 2015. Along with Joao Da Silva, he formed the Da Silva & Lino music production company. The duo are also the owners and founders of DS&L Music Group which hold interests in a number of businesses worldwide.

Over the past decade, the company has produced numerous commercially successful discography which include “Still Standing” by Anything But Monday, “La Criminal” by Fulanito, “Move Your Body” by 740 Boyz, and “Pump It Up” by Danzel, which earned worldwide sales of more than three million copies. They are also noted for their contributions in the success of the critically-acclaimed single “Hellgate” which peaked at the top ten of the Release Promo Hype Chart, a renowned ranking of club hits worldwide that are determined by disc jockeys and several important figures in the music industry.

Aside from music production, Nelson Lino and Joao Da Silva also offer A&R Consultation Services to various artists as well as record labels. They also work as executives at Beatsleaze Music and Renno Records. They are tasked to find new talents as well as to oversee the development of some artists.
