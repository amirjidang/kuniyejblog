Who is Henry Dwight Sedgwick V?

Henry Dwight Sedgwick V is a venture capitalist. He was the founder of Sedgwick Associates. He was born to Robert Minturn Sedgwick and Helen (Peabody) Sedgwick. Henry was also the father of American Actress Kyra Sedgwick.

He worked for ten years at the Aluminum Company of Canada in Montreal, Quebec. He then went on to become a Democratic district leader in New York City, New York, United States of America. Later, he worked in venture capital and eventually founded his own “Sedgwick Associates.”

Early Life

Henry was born on September 13, 1926, in Boston, Suffolk County, Massachusetts, USA. He was brought up in the Massachusetts towns of Dedham and Groton. As per his date of birth, he belongs to the Virgo zodiac sign. He is an American by nationality.

He died in Essex, Middlesex County, Connecticut, on April 24, 2018, and was buried in Stockbridge, Massachusetts.

He is the son of Helen (Peabody) (mother) and Robert Minturn Sedgwick (father). Henry is also the brother of May Minturn Sedgwick and Frances Helen Sedgwick.

Education

Henry attended Harvard University and graduated in 1951. When he played football and hockey for Harvard, he was known as “Duke.” He then went to Geneva, Switzerland’s Centre d’Etudes Industriel.

Professional Career

Henry Dwight Sedgwick V worked for ten years at the Aluminum Company of Canada in Montreal, Quebec. He then went on to become a Democratic district leader in New York City, New York, United States of America. Later, he worked in venture capital and eventually founded his firm, “Sedgwick Associates.”

Relationships

According to some online sources, Henry was married three times in his lifetime. He was married to Helen Stern, Patricia Rosenwald and another private wife. He has a child from Patricia, Kyra Minturn Sedgwick (1965). Kyra is an actress. His other children are Robert Sedgwick, Holly Sedgwick and Mike Stern.

Net Worth
