Julianne Hough Works Her Toned Body in Striped Bikini (Plus That Ring)

Bicycles built for two recently engaged cuties! Julianne Hough modeled a teensy striped bikini and her ginormous engagement ring to pedal around Manhattan Beach, Calif., with her fiancé, Brooks Laich, on Saturday, Aug. 29.

The Dancing With the Stars judge, 27, showcased her toned dancer’s physique in a red and white two-piece, which she wore with nothing else (save for her 6-carat stunner). She went without a stitch of makeup on her tanned face. Meanwhile her hunky, hockey-playing hubby-to-be, 32, stood by her side in a white T-shirt and crimson O’Neill swim trunks.

Almost one week later, on Sept. 3, Hough, who’s nominated for a choreography Emmy for the upcoming awards show on Sept. 20, was snapped getting a manicure, when she ran into Full House alum Lori Loughlin. The two started chatting, and the blonde beauty showed off her ring.

Hough, who announced her engagement to Laich via social media on Aug. 18, hasn’t been able to stop gushing about her Lorraine Schwartz-designed bauble since the day she received it, including on her personal website.
