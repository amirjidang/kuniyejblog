10 Celebrities Who Have A Non-Famous Twin

The world knows its fair share of celebrity twins. Among the most famous are the Olsen twins, and Beyoncé's twins Rumi and Sir Carter. While fans want to know everything about their favorite celebrity, they rarely extend that interest to their siblings. What they often never realize is that their favorite celebrity has a twin sibling.

Related: 10 Little Known Facts About Eminem's Family

It's hard to imagine that there is an identical copy of certain celebrities walking around somewhere and living a life away from the spotlight. Most A-listers have a non-identical twin though, and sometimes, they barely resemble one another.

10 Eva Green

Eva Green is most famous as the Bond girl from Casino Royale (2006), but she has since appeared in several movies and TV shows, such as Miss Peregrine's Home for Peculiar Children (2016) and Penny Dreadful.

The 40 year-old French actress has a non-identical twin sister, named Joy Green. According to Hello Magazine, she lives in Italy and runs a wine company together with her husband. Eva even joked that she is the other, happier version of herself.

9 Kiefer Sutherland

Both Kiefer and his twin sister Rachel work in the entertainment industry. She works as a post-production film supervisor. Among her most recent projects are Nurses (2020) and Mary Kills People (2017).

The Sutherland twins were born in London, but they later moved to Canada as both of their parents are actually Canadian. They both have a dual citizenship.

8 Ashton Kutcher

Born in the Midwest, Ashton Kutcher started out as a model and only later moved to Hollywood and built his acting career. His twin brother Michael is mostly known for being related to Ashton, but he is also a businessman and a public speaker.

He advocates for people with cerebral palsy, the permanent movement disorder he was diagnosed with at the age of three. When he was thirteen years old, he almost died from cardiac arrest. He was lucky, though: he had a successful heart transplant.

7 Scarlett Johansson

Scarlett Johansson has a twin brother, named Hunter. When she's wearing makeup, the resemblance is not as obvious as on the pictures where she went makeup-free.

Hunter has a heart of gold. He founded Solar Responders, a charity that responds to natural disasters. When Hurricane Maria devastated Puerto Rico, the organization provided solar panels to help the country get back on its feet.

6 Rami Malek

The Bohemian Rhapsody star has an identical twin brother whose name rhymes with his: they are Rami and Sami Malek. They also have an older sister Yasmine and she is a doctor.

They were born to Egyptian Coptic Orthodox immigrants. Sami lives in LA and works as a teacher. When the twins were younger, it was really hard to tell them apart. Rami once took a test as Sami and nobody noticed.

5 Giovanni Ribisi

Giovanni and Marissa Ribisi are both well-known in their circles, but Giovanni is still the one twin that will more likely ring a bell. On television, he portrayed Frank Buffay, Jr.  in Friends, and Jeff Billings in The Wonder Years. His filmography is also impressive; he appeared in Avatar (2009), Ted (2012), and Selma (2014).

His twin sister Marissa also appeared in a number of TV shows in all kinds of minor roles. In 2007, she launched her own fashion line Whitley Kros.

4 Jon Heder

Jon Heder is most famous for his role in Napoleon Dynamite (2004) and Blades of Glory (2007). He grew up in a big family. Besides his identical twin Dan, Heder has four siblings.

It seems that entertainment runs in both of their veins. Dan is a visual effects artist who worked on a lot of hit movies, such as Guardians of the Galaxy (2014), Captain America: Civil War (2016), and Spider-Man: Far from Home (2019).

3 Gisele Bundchen

It's not just yoga that's keeping Gisele Bundchen forever young, it's also good genes. The Brazilian model has five sisters, one of which is her fraternal twin. Just like Gisele, her twin sister Patricia and sister Gabriela also gave modeling a shot. Gisele was the only who who got signed by Elite Model Management, though.

The supermodel didn't leave her twin behind; Patricia worked as her manager and spokesperson.

2 Vin Diesel

Vin Diesel has an adorable family. He is a father of three and is married to Paloma Jimenez, a Mexican model. He is good at keeping his personal life private. After all, most fans don't even know what's his real name.

The Fast and Furious star was born as Mark Sinclair in California and he grew up in New York City. His twin brother is a film editor and he doesn't really look much like Diesel. His name is Paul Vincent.

1 Parker Posey

The 52-year old Parker Posey is most famous for her work with Christopher Guest, appearing in many of his mockumentaries, such as For Your Consideration (2006) and Mascots (2016). She's also appeared in a number of indie movies in the '90s, such as The Daytrippers (1996) and The House of Yes (1997).

Parker has a twin brother, named Christopher who is a lawyer.
