Hot 97 DJ Mister Cee Arrested After Soliciting Male Prostitute

Hot 97 DJ and famed hip-hop producer Mister Cee was arrested in Brooklyn on Friday night, after he solicited a male undercover cop for sex. The cop was posing as a prostitute and was approached by Mister Cee, who asked “How much?,” according to reports.

The incident occurred in the Bedford Stuyvesant neighborhood of Brooklyn. Cee, real name Calvin Lebron, will reportedly talk about the incident on Hot 97 Monday (5/6). This is not the first time that the host of Hot 97’s Throwback at Noon and Friday Night Live has been booked for something of this nature. He’s been arrested several times for loitering for the purpose of prostitution, and, in 2011, he was handcuffed after police found him in his car getting fellated by a 20-year-old male. Cee denied the whole thing and insisted that “that shit not true.”

Lots of people will be tuned into Throwback at Noon on Monday to hear what Cee has to say this time.
