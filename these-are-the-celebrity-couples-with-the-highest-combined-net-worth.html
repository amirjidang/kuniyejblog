These Are The 10 Celebrity Couples With The Highest Combined Net Worth

It's no secret that celebrity couples are extremely wealthy and that their combined net worths are usually incredibly high because both of them make a lot of money. Today's list is exactly about that — the richest celebrity couples in the industry. While it is often the case that one celebrity makes a lot more money than their partner — their combined net worths are still very impressive.

From Hollywood couples like Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds to iconic musicians such as Beyoncé and Jay-Z — keep on scrolling to see just who made the list!

10 Blake Lively And Ryan Reynolds — Combined Net Worth $180 Million

Kicking the list off are Hollywood stars Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds who have a combined net worth of $180 million. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Ryan Reynolds's net worth is $150 million while Blake Lively's net worth is $30 million. The couple met in 2010 and a year later they began dating. In 2012 Blake and Ryan got married and together the couple has three daughters.

9 Mila Kunis And Ashton Kutcher — Combined Net Worth $275 Million

Let's move on to actors Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher who together have a combined net worth of $275 million. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Ashton Kutcher has a net worth of $200 million while his wife Mila is estimated to have a net worth of $75 million. The couple — who knew each other from the set of That '70s Show — started dating in 2012. In 2015 Ashton and Mila got married and currently, they have two children together.

8 Justin And Hailey Bieber — Combined Net Worth $305 Million

Next are musician Justin Bieber and model Hailey Baldwin who together have a combined net worth of $305 million.

7 Nicole Kidman And Keith Urban — Combined Net Worth $325 Million

Another famous couple that made it onto today's list is actress Nicole Kidman and musician Keith Urban. Nicole and Keith met in 2005 and a year later the two decided to tie the knot. 6 Will Smith And Jada Pinkett Smith —Combined Net Worth $400 Million

Let's move on to yet another famous Hollywood couple — this time we're talking about Will Smith and Jada Pinkett Smith. Will and Jada met back in the 90s on the set of The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air and in 1997 the couple got married. Together the two have two children. According to Celebrity Net Worth, their combined net worth is $400 million — Will's net worth is $350 million while Jada's net worth is $50 million.

5 David And Victoria Beckham — Combined Net Worth $450 Million

Let's move on to David and Victoria Beckham who, according to Celebrity Net Worth, have a combined net worth of $450 million. The former member of the Spice Girls met the former professional soccer player in 1997 at a charity soccer match — and in 1999 the couple got married. Together, David and Victoria Beckham have four children.

4 George And Amal Clooney — Combined Net Worth $550 Million

Next on the list are Hollywood star George Clooney and human rights lawyer Amal Alamuddin. According to Celebrity Net Worth, the couple has a combined net worth of $550 million.
