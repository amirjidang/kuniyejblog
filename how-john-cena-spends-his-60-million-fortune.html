How John Cena Spends His $60 Million Fortune

John Cena has earned a multi-million dollar net worth from wrestling and acting in movies over the last two decades. The celebrity loves to spend his millions buying expensive collectibles.

John Cena came into the public eye in 2000 when he began his professional career as a wrestler. He won the WWE Championship in 2005 and became a high-profile wrestler for his talent inside the ring. The wrestling champion has won over 15 titles and improved his acting career. Cena has starred in actions movies such as Suicide Squad and The Fast Saga. He also ventured into comedy that showed his talent in Trainwreck, Vacation Friends, and Dolittle. The actor has earned millions from his movie roles, along with winning championships. He has managed to amass a $60 million fortune until now.

From buying vintage cars to wearing stylish clothes, John Cena loves to spend his net worth on things that matter. The wrestler/actor loves to stay on top of his game for the cameras. With a sprawling net worth at his disposal, let’s take a look at how John Cena spends his money.

Real Estate

While John Cena leads a relatively private life, everyone got a glimpse into his home while dating the TV personality Nikki Bella. Cena owns a home in Tampa, Florida. Located in a community in Land O’ Lakes, he purchased it in 2005 for $525,000. The star has made several modifications to his home over the years. The home is now worth $3.6 million, featuring five bedrooms and four bathrooms. The home has indoor and outdoor swimming pools. The indoor pool features two slides and a waterfall. The most recent renovations were made to the home in 2018 when he reconstructed the garage and put a massive steel fence around his home. After marrying Shay Shariatzadeh in 2020, he purchased a home in the Mission Hills in San Diego, California.

Stylish Wardrobe

John Cena loves his suits. While he is known for wearing a printed t-shirt and jean shorts in the wrestling ring, he loves to wear suits when he is doing promotions, press junket, and walking the red carpet. On an episode of The Bellas, Cena confessed that he purchased 55 suits at once when he was on a trip to New York. In an interview with GQ, he revealed that he liked suits because they made a nice statement. He also revealed that his stylish wardrobe containing suits was inspired by his mentor and former wrestler Vince McMahon.

Car Collection

Known for his tough personality on screen and in the wrestling ring, it is no surprise that John Cena loves muscle cars. The star has a niche and artistic collection, which shows that he knows the beauty of vehicles. He owned a Chevy Corvette ZR1 and was the 73rd person to buy the rare automobile. Some other vehicles include a 1970 Plymouth Road Runner Superbird, Buick GSX, AMC Rebel ‘The Machine,’ Corvette InCenaRator, Lincoln Continental, Ford GT, Ferrari F430 Spider, Lamborghini Gallardo LP560-4, Dodge Charger Hemi 426, 1969 Chevrolet COPO Camaro, 1970 Mercury Cougar Eliminator, and a Jeep Wrangler, as stated by Hot Cars.

Private Jet Rides

Even though John Cena is a part-time wrestler now, he is still the face of WWE. As the most celebrated wrestler, the star has a comparatively higher salary than other wrestlers. He makes $8.5 million through his WWE salary annually, and his contract allows him access to travel by private jets. During press junkets and promotions, he travels in a private jet. The charges to charter a private jet start from $3,000 an hour when using a smaller jet.

WrestleMania Entrances

When John Cena steps foot into the WWE Arena, it is nothing short of a spectacle. WrestleMania is the biggest night for WWE superstars. An extravagance of entertainment, wrestlers go all out on expenses to create the best entrance that the audience will remember. John Cena currently holds the record for the most expensive entrance for WrestleMania 23, as stated by The Travel. The venue was Detroit, Michigan, and Cena drove through the field in a Ford Mustang before crashing through the Mania logo's glass. The setup cost him a whopping $100,000.

Philanthropy

John Cena has always been a caring soul who loves to help people. In 2011, he mentioned on Twitter that he had 9 million flying miles and donated them to the Make-A-Wish Foundation, and in 2013, he gave away 4.5 million flying miles. The wrestler has won several accolades for his charity work, including the Chris Greicius Celebrity Award. According to The Sun, Cena is the brand ambassador for Make-A-Wish Foundation and has granted over 6000 wishes as he is the most requested celebrity. He has once paid for army veterans’ groceries and donated $1 million to FitOps Foundation that trains retired veterans.

John Cena does the best of both worlds, from owning an expensive wardrobe and homes to collecting cars and making generous donations. The star has a down-to-earth personality and loves to spend his millions on the finer things in life. Cena will next be seen reprising his role as Peacemaker from Suicide Squad for his DC series Peacemaker, which will grace the screens in 2022.
