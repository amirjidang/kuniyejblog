Anne Thompson NBC Age: Does She Have A Breast Cancer?

The 67 years old NBC journalist Anne Thompson is a founder of the Thompson on Hollywood blog. Stick to this article to know about her.

Anne started her journalism career in 1979 after joining WNDU-TV based in South Bend, Indiana. She worked there for four years till 1983.

She later became a consumer reporter for NBC affiliate KSDK-TV based in Saint Louis, Missouri. She held the position till 1986.

Her health declined a few years ago. She was diagnosed with breast cancer.

Does Anne Thompson Have A Breast Cancer?

Yes, the American journalist got Breast Cancer. In 2006, she was diagnosed with stage 3 breast cancer.

She wanted to keep her diagnosis private. She didn’t want her to be a story for people. She said;

“If my appearance changed dramatically and I didn’t look good, I didn’t want to be on air because that would detract from my story.”

But the chemotherapy let her hair loose, eyebrows, and eyelashes too. For this, she used to wear two wigs and nicked named “Mata Hari”.

The brave journalist then decided to speak publicly about her diagnosis. Her article on cancer was published in Coping magazine in 2007.

After conquering the battle with cancer, she still did so well in her profession. She won the Rachel Carson Award in 2017.

She also won the News & Documentary Emmy Award for outstanding Live coverage of a Current News Story -Long Form.

Anne Thompson Health Condition – Know about her illness

Anne Thompson beat cancer. She might have understood how life is unpredictable with illness.

So she now is doing things she might have done earlier like climbing Australia’s Sydney Harbour Bridge though she is scared of heights.

However, she accepts that the fear of cancer hitting her again is always there. But still, she is trying and realizing life can’t be lived in fear.

She says, “I decided that instead of living with fear, I was going to live fearlessly.
