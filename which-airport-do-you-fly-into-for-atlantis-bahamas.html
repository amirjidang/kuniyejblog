What is the closest airport to Atlantis?

What is the nearest airport to Atlantis Paradise Island? The nearest airport to Atlantis Paradise Island is Nassau (NAS) Airport which is 15.1 km away.

How far is Atlantis from Nassau airport?

Yes, the driving distance between Nassau Airport (NAS) to Atlantis Paradise Island is 15 km. It takes approximately 15 min to drive from Nassau Airport (NAS) to Atlantis Paradise Island.

How do I get to Atlantis from the airport?

Flying into Nassau International Airport is the most convenient way to get to Atlantis. Taxis are readily available at Nassau International to take passengers on the 15-mile trip to Atlantis. Passengers can purchase transfers to and from the airport through Atlantis when booking vacations through the Atlantis website.

How much is a taxi from Nassau Airport to Atlantis?

The base cab fare from Nassau International Airport to Harborside at Atlantis on Paradise Island is $38 for the first two passengers, $4 for each additional passenger, $2 for the Paradise Island Bridge toll, and $2 for each extra bag. Plus tip, of course.

Can I use US dollars in Bahamas?

The US $ is of equal value with the Bahamian $, US currency is accepted for all transactions and can be used with Bahamian currency. Be sure to spend whatever Bahamian bills you have before departure, as it’s nearly impossible to exchange outside the Bahamas.

Do US cell phones work in the Bahamas?

The Bahamas uses the Area Code 242. … Many US and Canadian carriers have roaming agreements with Batelco, the GSM cellular operator in the Bahamas. Therefore, while most phones will work, the rates are approximately $3.00 per minute plus tax for all in and out calls.

Can you drink the water in the Bahamas?

Drinking tap water in the Bahamas is generally of good quality and safety is considered to be well regulated. However, as in any hot tropical country, there are still some precautions that wise visitors prefer to take. First of all, although tap water is considered good, you can still stick to bottled water.

Is Jamaica or Bahamas better?

Can I use my debit card in the Bahamas?

Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and most debit cards are also widely accepted throughout Nassau Paradise Island, as well as travelers’ cheques. Some credit card companies may charge small international transaction fees, so it’s best to check with your provider before leaving home.

What is the most popular dish in the Bahamas?

Conch Seafood. Seafood is a staple in the Bahamas. Conch, a large tropical mollusk (sea snail) with firm, white flesh, is the national dish of the Bahamas. Conch can be prepared in a number of ways: served raw with lime juice, raw vegetables and even fruit called conch salad.

How much cash can I bring to the Bahamas?

$10,000 The Bahamas Parliament has enacted the Travellers Currency Declaration Act of 2014 which requires travelers to declare upon entry or exit if in possession of $10,000 or more in any currency.

Do you tip in Bahamas?

In the Bahamas, service staff and hotel workers expect to be tipped. … Many hotels and restaurants automatically add a 15% gratuity to your bill; if not, a 15% to 20% tip at a restaurant is appropriate (more for a high-end establishment). Bartenders generally get $1 to $2 per drink.

What is the best currency to use in the Bahamas?

If you are coming from the United States, the best currency to use in the Bahamas is the U.S. dollar. It just makes it simpler not to have to exchange. As we’ve discussed, the Bahamian dollar is equally linked to the U.S. dollar in value.

What can you not bring to The Bahamas?

Meat, provisions, fruits and vegetables, and any articles intended for human food which are unfit for human consumption. Indecent or obscene prints, paintings, photographs, books, cards, lithographic or other engravings, or any other indecent or obscene article.

Can you take alcohol to Bahamas?

You can bring in one quart of alcohol, which includes spirits and wine. The Bahamas applies a duty to all pint. At the time of publication, the rates are $18 per case or $10 for an imperial gallon. You can bring personal electronics, valued at $100 or less, duty-free, but customs limits you to two cameras.
