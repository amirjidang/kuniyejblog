Don West Cause of Death

American pitchman and wrestling broadcaster, Don West has died. He died on December 30, 2022, at the age of 59.

On June 2021, West announced that he had been diagnosed with lymphoma of the brain, and began treatment. He died from lymphoma at the age of 59.

West previously worked as a host for the shop At Home Network. In the 1990s, West achieved a degree of fame for his loud, energetic, abrasive sales pitches on late-night television, and his blunt obvious observations, with his persuasive style.

On January 13, 2017, Impact Wrestling revealed that West had joined the merchandise department. On July 2, 2017, West returned to the commentary XV pay-per-view.
