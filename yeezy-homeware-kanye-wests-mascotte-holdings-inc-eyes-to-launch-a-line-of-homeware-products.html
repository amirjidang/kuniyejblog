Yeezy Homeware? Kanye West's Mascotte Holdings, Inc. Eyes To Launch A Line of Homeware Products

If you're a Kanye fan, you know that music is never the only thing the hip-hop mogul has on his plate. From his ten-year clothing deal with GAP to his 2020 campaign to take over the Oval Office, Kanye West always has something in the middle while he's cooking up the next Billboard Top 100 hit song.

Now, fresh off the heels of his massively well-received album Donda, it looks like Kanye's setting out for his next big business venture: starting his very own line of homeware products.

RELATED: Kanye West Drums Up Millions At Donda's Third Event, Before Album Even Drops

West is no stranger to the finer aspects of interior design. He displayed his keen eye for decorating a space when he designed his ex-wife Kim Kardashian's Hidden Hills estate.

So it's only right that he's directing his attention towards producing luxury home items. West's company, Mascotte Holdings, Inc, recently filed a trademark application to start up the homeware line under the name of "Kanye West".

According to documents obtained by TMZ, the first to break the exclusive story, the line will include "shower curtains, textile wall hangings, towels, placemats and blankets."

The documents suggest that blankets will be a big selling point of the line potential catalog. From blankets for the shower to blankets for the bedroom, the paperwork shows that West is looking to dominate the blanket game. The documents go on to include potential fabrics like cashmere and fleece.

Though, it looks like West isn't the only celeb looking to get into the home decor business. In September of last year, then-wife Kim filed for the trademark "KKW Home" in an effort to sell her own luxury household essentials. She's looking to get her own bath and shower products on the shelves. Though when the couple separated earlier this year, she opted for a different name - "SKNN BY KIM".

On top of being one of the most streamed artists in the world, West has gained his $1.8 billion net worth from a number of different ventures over the years. Just last year, his Yeezy shoes brought in sales of around $1.7 billion. Also, in 2020, West signed a deal with GAP, and the Yeezy clothing line hit the shelves.

We'll just have to wait and see if the 'Kanye West' homeware line lives up to his billion-dollar potential.
