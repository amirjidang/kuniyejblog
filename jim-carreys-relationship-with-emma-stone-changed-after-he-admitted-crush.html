Jim Carrey's Relationship With Emma Stone Changed After He Awkwardly Admitted She Was His Crush

Highlights

  • Jim Carrey confessed his affection for Emma Stone in a humorous video.
  • Emma Stone was flattered by Carrey's confession and expressed her admiration for him.
  • Both Carrey and Stone have been involved in high-profile relationships.

Jim Carrey left fans wondering what the connection was back in 2011 when he took out time to express his affection for star actress Emma Stone. The most shocking part of the video was how the A-list comedian's words were direct, intentional, and humorous in their entirety. While many fans were left with mixed feelings, the recipient was impressed and handled it pleasantly.

The Mask actor's video caused quite an uproar among social media users, but it was a reciprocation of admiration when Emma Stone replied. Jim Carrey has had his fair share of controversies, and this was one of those moments. While he stated that he fancied her, he also acknowledged their significant age difference, but did that hinder the lovely exchange from becoming something more intense? Here's all the update after Carrey confessed his feelings to Stone.

Where Does Emma Stone And Jim Carrey's Relationship Stand Since His Crush Confession?

At the time, the Dumb and Dumber actor professed his feelings for the Cruella actress, she was romantically involved with Andrew Garfield. Although Carrey's message was a funny yet affectionate one, it did not lead to a romance between both parties. It rather elicited a sweet reaction from Emma, making the confession appear as light-hearted fun between the duo.

Also, the series of events following Jim Carrey's confession to Emma Stone proved they didn't go beyond platonic. According to DailyMail, in the same week that the star actor reached out to The Help actress, she and her boyfriend, Andrew Garfield, were seen enjoying a romantic evening in Malibu. The partners looked pretty much loved-up as they dined at Nobu. Garfield and Stone's relationship was relatively new at the time, as they met on the set of The Amazing Spider-Man in June 2011. Garfield also did not appear to be troubled or concerned after Carrey made his confession.

On Jim Carrey's part, less than two months after putting out the eye-opening video, the Bruce Almighty actor was spotted with an unknown blonde. He appeared to have moved past his admitted infatuation with Stone as he walked out of a building in New York City in October 2011. Carrey was previously in a committed relationship with actress Jenny McCarthy. The pair dated from 2005 to 2010, and they had a son together.

How Jim Carrey Confessed His Love For Emma Stone

The How The Grinch Stole Christmas actor piqued fans' curiosity on YouTube after he shared a video of himself on his website, Jim Carrey Tru Life. The star made an up-close clip that appeared to have been filmed indoors as he addressed Emma Stone. Carrey started by stating that his message was for Stone. Per THR, he began:

"Emma, I just wanted to let you know that I think you're all the way beautiful. Not just pretty, but you know, smart and kind-hearted. And if I were a lot younger, I would marry you, and we would have chubby little freckle-faced kids."

The actor went on to gush about all the fun they would have together if they were a couple. He mentioned, 'We'd laugh all day long, go camping, play Yahtzee, tell ghost stories by the fire..." Jim Carrey's words turned suggestive as he mentioned, 'And the sex..." and at that point he trailed off into a dramatic pause. The Batman Forever actor continued wooing Stone by envisioning how they would spend their lives together. However, he veered off to mention how their age gap made the difference. Carrey relayed:

Social media users did not hold back their mixed feelings about how the actor addressed the then-23-year-old actress. Many found the clip creepy as they tried to decipher if it was just for laughs. However, Emma Stone did not share the same sentiment. She was rather flattered by the admiration. The La La Land actress told New York Magazine:

"I was flattered, I can't even tell you. Honest! I was really flattered, I really was."

The redhead beauty went on to reveal how she and some stars talked about Jim Carrey at the MTV Movie Awards shortly before he released the video. Emma Stone recalled how she, Jason Sudeikis, and some other colleagues discussed his impressive career. She revealed:

Jim Carrey would later clarify why he put out the video on social media. He took to Twitter (X) addressing fans and letting them know it was from a humorous angle. The pair met on SNL in 2016, and confirmed their relationship the following year. Stone welcomed a baby daughter with him in 2021.
