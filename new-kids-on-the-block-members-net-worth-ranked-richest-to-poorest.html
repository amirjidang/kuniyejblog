Every Member Ranked From Richest To Poorest

Highlights

  • Mark Wahlberg transitioned from the boy band into a successful acting and producing career, accumulating a net worth of $400 million.
  • Donnie Wahlberg continues to star on Blue Bloods and host TV shows, earning a reported $150,000 per episode starring on the show.
  • Joey McIntyre is now on Broadway and Jordan Knight co-owns a restaurant, and both have hefty net worths.

During their prime, the New Kids on the Block (NKOTB) were very popular, especially with the girls, known as "Blockheads." The teen heartthrobs had hits like Step by Step, Hangin' Tough, and You Got It (The Right Stuff). Their dance moves became iconic, too. For a full decade, they were one of the most popular musical acts on the radio waves. Long before N'Sync, Backstreet Boys and One Direction were all anyone could talk about, it was Jordan, Joey, Donnie, Danny, and Jonathan.

The group did take a break in 1994, but they got back together in 2007 and continued to tour and play together. NKOTB is still one of the most famous boy bands in pop music history, leaving a huge mark on the entertainment industry. So where are they now?

Keep reading as we go over the New Kids on the Block members' net worths, and maybe some other interesting things you didn't know about them!

6 Mark Wahlberg

Net Worth: $400 Million

  • Mark Walhberg went on to have a rap career and is now a successful actor and producer

You're probably thinking we mean Donnie Wahlberg, but before Marky Mark and the Funky Bunch, Mark Wahlberg was an original member of NKOTB. Only for a few months, so he never made it onto the hit videos or top-selling albums, but nevertheless, he was a member.

Mark Wahlberg, as mentioned, then transformed himself into a teen rapper. As the frontman of Marky Mark and the Funky Bunch, he rose to fame for his music, including the hit single "Good Vibrations." His shirtless Calvin Klein ads and chiseled physique also made him a prominent figure in the fashion world.

Mark Wahlberg's Transition Into Acting Added To His Net Worth

Throughout the 1990s, Mark Wahlberg transitioned into acting. He appeared in several films, including The Basketball Diaries (1995), Three Kings (1999), and The Perfect Storm (2000). His acting talent was phenomenal from the start, and it was obvious he had found his calling.

Wahlberg expanded his career by becoming a successful producer, with credits on projects like Entourage (2004–2011) and Boardwalk Empire (2010–2014). He ventured into entrepreneurship, owning various businesses, including a restaurant chain called Wahlburgers.

Throughout the years, Mark Wahlberg has risen up to the ranks of 'highest paid actor in Hollywood' and continued making, producing, and acting in films. In fact, he has had quite a few movies gross over $100 million at the box office. To this day, he is still a superstar and definitely the most famous member of the previous boy band.

Mark's net worth is $400 million.

5 Donnie Wahlberg

Net Worth: $25 Million

  • Donnie Wahlberg stars on Blue Bloods when not touring with NKOTB

Donnie Wahlberg, the older brother of Mark Wahlberg, was a much bigger part of New Kids on the Block. He was the one with the huge stage presence and great backing vocals and rap skills. Did you know he was also in a rap group? The Kool-Aid Bun didn't go far, but for high school kids, they had drive and ambition.

Donnie was 15 years old when he auditioned to be in NKOTB. Donnie did all the albums and tours with the group, unlike his brother, Mark.

After the band split, Donnie pursued acting. His first movie was Bullet, which starred Tupac Shakur and Mickey Rourke and came out in 1996. He then played a robber in Ransom with Mel Gibson the same year.

Donnie Wahlberg has a net worth of $25 million.

What Is Donnie Wahlberg Doing Now?

Donnie Wahlberg has had a number of career achievements since being in the boy band. He plays Detective Daniel Reagan on the massive hit TV show Blue Bloods, which started in 2010. He was also on his brother's reality show Wahlburgers in 2014. However, of the two, Blue Bloods is what has really padded his bank account. In fact, everyone on Blue Bloods receives an enormous paycheck.

Donnie started making $60,000 per episode and, more recently, has been making $150,000 per episode, which adds up to about $3.3 million a season.

Donnie Wahlberg also hosts a show on Investigation Discovery (ID) called Very Scary People. This is a show that talks about some of the most cringy and creepy serial killers of all time. It has been a huge success, and for his part, Donnie has done a fantastic job throughout the five seasons.

4 Joey McIntyre

Net Worth: $25 Million

  • Joey McIntyre stars on Broadway when not touring with New Kids on the Block

Joey McIntyre was one of the lead singers in New Kids. One song in particular, "Please Don't Go Girl," really put him and the band on the map. He was the youngest member of the band too, joining at just 13 years old.

After the initial disbandment, Joey pursued a solo music career, releasing four albums and acting in various television and stage roles.

Joey McIntyre's net worth is $25 million.

What Is Joey McIntyre Doing Now?

It's been decades since Joey McIntyre became famous as a solo artist or with New Kids on the Block. These days, you can still find him on the stage; it's just on Broadway. He has been in "Waitress" and "Tick, Tick... BOOM!"

In 2022, he was the star of "The Wanderer" in its pre-Broadway run.

Joey was also married in 2003 to Barrett Williams, and they have three children together.

3 Jordan Knight

Net Worth: $18 Million

  • Jordan Knight is co-owner of the restaurant Novara

New Kids on the Block's lead singer was Jordan Knight. He was also the group's most popular member.

Jordan Knight's net worth is $18 million.

Even after they broke up, Jordan Knight kept making songs, both as a solo artist and with NKOTB. He put out solo records like "Unfinished" and "Nick & Knight," which was a collaboration with Backstreet Boys' Nick Carter. Jordan also kept busy by traveling and performing with the band.

What Is Jordan Knight Doing Now?

According to the NOKTB.blog (die-hard fans who keep close tabs on the boys), Jordan Knight lives in Milton (MA) with his wife and two sons.

Since 2016, Jordan Knight has been one of the investors and co-owners of an Italian restaurant in East Milton Square called Novara.

2 Danny Wood

Net Worth: $16 Million

  • Danny Wood posts fitness videos, as well as runs TorchBearer Sauces when not touring

Danny Wood was seen as the big guy in the group. He sang bass, and he was great at it. He also put together most of the band's choreography, which was incredible.

Wanny Wood's net worth is $16 million.

What Is Danny Wood Doing Now?

Danny Wood is somewhat of a fitness pro currently. He posts a lot of exercise, bodybuilding, and healthy recipe videos on his social media. Wood also has a line of sauces called TorchBearer Sauces. These are heavy on the guacamole but include other flavors that pair remarkably well with it.

In honor of his late mother, Danny Wood also runs a charity called Remember Betty. The foundation raises money to help breast cancer patients and survivors directly.

Wood was also married for a few years before getting divorced in 2006. During that marriage, he had three children.

1 Jonathan Knight

Net Worth: $14 Million

  • Jonathan Knight hosts Farmhouse Fixer when not touring with NKOTB

Jonathan Knight is the brother of Jordan Knight. He was quieter than the others and sang harmony in the group.

Jonathan Knight's net worth is $14 million.

Jonathan Knight was the first member of New Kids on the Block to leave before they 'officially' broke up in 1994. After he stopped playing in the band, Knight worked as an investor in real estate and rarely went out in public. He later said that his anxiety was a big reason why he chose to step away from the press.

What Is Jonathan Knight Doing Now?

In 2008, Jonathan Knight felt ready to start performing again, so he joined New Kids on the Block again for their reunion tours. But, besides singing, Knight has also been on a few reality TV shows, such as The Amazing Race and Farmhouse Fixer, where he fixes up old New England farmhouses. On a personal level, Knight was outed as being gay without his permission. In 2009, Knight's ex-boyfriend wrote a story for the National Enquirer in which he told everyone that Jonathan was gay.

Knight later told CNN that he wasn't ashamed of it, but when the band was at the height of their fame, the band's manager knew about it and told him to hide it. He said he told him that Sony would lose a lot of money and that his and the band's career would end if anyone found out.

Jonathan Knight and Harley Rodriguez have been together since 2008. After getting engaged in 2016, they got married.
