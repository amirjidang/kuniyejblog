Several pairs of highly talented celebrity brother-sisters have taken Hollywood by storm.

The saying that ‘ Talent Runs In The Family ’ has been proved right time and again in Hollywood in the form of highly successful sibling duos. There are currently several brother-sister duos in Hollywood who have successfully built a career for themselves in various fields like acting, music, modeling, and filmmaking, earning millions of dollars in the process. Some siblings are constantly seen sharing screen space or collaborating across various platforms.

The most well-known and richest brother-sister duos in Hollywood include the likes of Peter and Jane Fonda, Julia and Eric Roberts, Jake, and Maggie Gyllenhaal, and the list goes on. Below is a list ranking the richest brother-sister duos in Hollywood.

Jason and Justine Bateman - $36 Million

Justine and Jason Bateman have excelled in their fields over their long careers. Children of the director and producer Kent Bateman, Jason is a successful Emmy and Golden Globe-winning actor and director known for his work in Hancock, Zootopia, Game Night, Ozark, etc. At the same time, his elder sister Justine is a successful actress currently engaged in filmmaking with her directorial short film Five Minutes premiering at the 2017 Toronto International Film Festival. The duo has a collective net worth of $36 Million, with Jason’s net worth being $30 Million and Justine’s being $6 Million as per Celebrity Net Worth.

Evan Ross and Tracee Ellis Ross - $41 Million

The children of acclaimed singer Diane Ross, Evan Ross, and Tracee Ellis Ross have made a name for themselves as successful actors. Evan Ross is known for his appearances in movies like ATL, Supremacy, The Hunger Games: Mockingjay Part 1 and 2, etc., and he has followed in his mother's footsteps to become a successful musician too. Tracee Ellis Ross is known for her lead roles in TV shows like Black-ish and Girlfriends. Evan and Tracee have an impressive net worth of $25 Million and $16 Million, respectively, taking their combined net worth to $41 Million.

David and Patricia Arquette - $54 Million

David Arquette is a successful actor and wrestler and is mostly known for his appearance as Dewey Riley in the 90s hit franchise Scream. Patricia Arquette is a renowned Academy Award-winning actress who has starred in some iconic movies, including A Nightmare on Elm Street 3: Dream Warriors, Boyhood, etc. The children of the late actor Lewis Arquette, the duo have tasted success in the industry and have accumulated a combined net worth of $54 Million, with Patricia contributing $30 Million and Patricia contributing $24 million as per Wealthy Gorilla.

John and Joan Cusack - $70 Million

John and Joan Cusack have co-starred in a record ten movies making them the sibling duo appearing in most films together, as reported by Insider. John and Joan have made it big in the industry, following the footsteps of their father and acclaimed filmmaker Dick Cusack. John is a Golden Globe-nominated actor best known for his work in the critically acclaimed Love and Mercy, while Joan is an Academy Award-nominated actress with movies like Working Girl and In & Out. The duo shares a dynamic on-screen chemistry, having appeared together in films like Sixteen Candles, Grosse Pointe Blank, War, Inc., etc. John has a net worth of $50 Million, while Joan has a net worth of $20 Million.

Jake And Maggie Gyllenhaal - $105 Million

Inheriting talent from their parents, director Stephen Gyllenhaal and screenwriter Naomi Foner, the brother-sister duo of Jake and Maggie Gyllenhaal, are both Academy Award-nominated actors. Jake was nominated for an Academy Award for Best actor for his role in Brokeback Mountain. He recently starred as the antagonist Mysterio in MCU’s Spider-Man: No Way Home. Just like her younger brother, Maggie Gyllenhaal is a critically acclaimed actress known for her role in Crazy Heart. Jake has amassed a staggering $80 Million net worth, while his older sister has a current net worth of $25 Million.

Shirley Maclaine and Warren Beatty - $120 Million

The most awarded and talented brother-sister duo on this list, Shirley Maclaine and Warren Beatty are both legends in their game. Shirley is a veteran actress and model with 6 Academy Award nominations and widespread filmography. Over a career spanning over seven decades, she has amassed a net worth of $50 Million. Multi-talented is an understatement for actor, director, and screenwriter Warren Beatty. He has a whopping 14 Oscar Nominations that too in 4 several categories over a career spanning six decades while amassing a net worth of $70 Million.

Peter and Jane Fonda - $240 Million

With a net worth of $40 Million and a whopping $200 Million, respectively, Peter and Jane Fonda are one of the richest brother-sister duos in the industry. They are the children of the legendary Hollywood actor John Fonda, so it is not wrong to say that acting runs through their blood. The late Peter Fonda was a two-time Academy Award-nominated actor and screenwriter starring in many movies in his long career. He, unfortunately, breathed his last in 2019. Jane Fonda was even more successful than Peter, with a career spanning more than six decades; she has received multiple accolades, including two Academy Awards, two British Academy Film Awards, seven Golden Globe Awards, and so on. At age 85, she is still carrying on the legacy of the Fonda family.

Julia And Eric Roberts - $259 Million

Julia Roberts was a global heartthrob and the highest-paid actress in the world during the 90s. She is still considered one of the greatest actresses to ever grace the big screen. She has multiple accolades to her name, which include an Academy Award, a British Academy Film Award, and three Golden Globe Awards. She has amassed a net worth of a whopping $250 Million owing to her global stardom and illustrious acting career. Julia’s older brother Eric Roberts might not be as popular as Julia. Still, he is also a critically acclaimed actor with multiple Golden Globe nominations and was nominated for an Academy Award for his work in 1985's Runaway Train. Eric has a modest net worth of $9 Million, as reported by Wealthy Genius, with Julia contributing a major chunk of the duo’s combined $259 Million fortune.

Hollywood is dominated by some iconic brother-sister duos, including some of the industry's most known and critically acclaimed artists. With their successful Hollywood careers, the duos have amassed fortunes worth million, with Julia and Eric Roberts standing out to be the richest brother-sister duo in Hollywood with a combined $259 Million net worth.
