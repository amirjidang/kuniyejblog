Kia Shine Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

In 1980, Nakia Shine Coleman (born October 17, 1980), better known by his stage name Kia Shine, was signed to Def Jam Records as a rapper, songwriter, record producer, and record executive. His most successful song, “Krispy,” reached at number 66 on the Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs list and at number 24 on the Hot Rap Tracks chart in the United Kingdom. In the past, he has collaborated with musicians like as B-Hoody and La Chat, Mike Jones and Yo Gotti, Kingpin Skinny Pimp and Nelly, Jim Jones and Blak Jak, as well as Wifey and others. In addition to Nakia Shine, Kinfolk, Kia Shine, and Kinfolk Jones are some of his aliases. A special edition of BET’s Rap City was presented by him in August 2006. He has inked a recording contract with Universal Records. Kinfolk Rap Hustlaz is run by Kia Shine, who is a co-CEO alongside rapper/CEO Jack Frost. After selling more than 200,000 CDs via his own label, Rap Hustlaz, Kia Shine was named the winner of the Southern Entertainment Award (S.E.A.) for best underground artist this year. He is often linked with Yo Gotti and Kingpin Skinny Pimp, among other artists.
