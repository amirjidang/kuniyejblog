Fernando Fiore (born in Argentina) (born July 9) is an Argentine Hispanic sportscaster who works on television. He is the host of Republica Deportiva and has served as the anchor for Univision’s coverage of the FIFA World Cup in 1998, 2002, 2006, and 2010 respectively. His previous work includes co-hosting the Univision travel programme Fuera de Serie with Sofa Vergara and being the original presenter of Lente Loco, which he hosted until he was replaced by Raymond Arrieta in 1997. In 1994, he appeared in the film “True Lies” as a member of the cast. In 1998, he also worked as a DJ for Caracol Radio. He is the author of The World Cup: The Ultimate Guide to the Greatest Sports Spectacle on the Planet, which was nominated for three Emmy Awards.
