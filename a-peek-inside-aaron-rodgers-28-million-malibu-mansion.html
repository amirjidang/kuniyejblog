A Peek Inside Aaron Rodgers' $28 Million Malibu Mansion

A quarterback for Green Bay Packers, Aaron Rodgers’ has been active in the NFL since 2005, earning over $200 Million net worth. Rodgers is one of the world’s highest-paid athletes and has a multi-million dollar real estate portfolio across the country, including a $5.13 million Del Mar mansion. He uses his massive net worth to buy some of the most unattainable luxuries in the world, including luxury homes and a splendid car collection, including an $8 Million Mercedes-Benz Maybach Exelero.

In 2020, Aaron Rodgers and his ex-girlfriend bought a $28 Million luxury mansion in the pristine area of Malibu. The beachfront villa offers a stunning view of the ocean and a private funicular to the beach.

The Property Previously Belonged To Robbie Williams

Celebrities have long considered Malibu to be their favorite location. In addition to having excellent beach access, it is only a few miles away from the crowd and chaos of Los Angeles. So, it is no surprise that this $28 Million house can attract a lot of celebrities. Aaron Rodgers and his then-girlfriend, race car driver Danica Patrick initially rented out the $28 Million Villa from British pop sensation Robbie Williams as reported by The Realtor. What was originally planned out for only a summer stay turned into the couple buying off the property in 2020 for a staggering $28 Million.

It is not precisely the couple’s fault, as anyone can fall for the Mediterranean-style villa. Aaron and Danica got separated later, but he went on to retain the ownership of the luxury mansion, as reported by Essentially Sports. Spread across half an acre of land. This property provides a 4636 sq. ft living space consisting of four bedrooms and four bathrooms.

A Paradise For Beach lovers

Aaron Rodgers’ swanky Malibu mansion features an excellent indoor-outdoor setup with several rooms with a stunning ocean view and specially crafted outdoor seating areas to enjoy the cresting waters of the deep-blue ocean. The common space in the mansion features ceiling-to-wall and wall-to-wall windows, providing a panoramic view of the ocean while letting in the calming ocean breeze. The villa is directly connected to the beach via a zigzag staircase and a private funicular.

The mansion is designed to take maximum advantage of its seaside location. It also features an oceanfront terrace lined up with comfortable seating and dining spaces if Aaron wants to dine outside. As if the stunning view from the terrace was not enough, the beach house also has a massive backyard with many sitting areas, an enormous pool, and shades to avoid sunburn during the day and space to host barbecues as the sun sets. Apart from the string of amenities, the backyard opens to the golden beach.

The Mansion's Two Separate Structures

Aaron Rodgers’ Mediterranean-style mansion has two separate sections: the three-story main house and an adjoining 2-bedroom guest house with a garage. The main home features four stunning bedrooms accompanied by equally exquisite bathrooms. Aaron’s main quarters feature an arched ceiling, muted color palette, as Lonny reported, and a breathtaking sea view that gets scaled up by a spacious balcony. The bathroom offers spa-like vibes with sliding glass doors and access to a private balcony.

The guest lounge is equally magnificent as the main house and features two stunning bedrooms featuring a rustic look with wood-paneled ceilings to a farmhouse-inspired accent wall passing which is quite different from the bedrooms in the main house. The bathrooms are on par with the main house and feature light wood accents, paneled glasses barriers, and stone countertops. And if all this luxury was not enough, the guest lounge also features a car garage that can fit three vehicles, as reported by The Sun.

The main house's interior features an open floor plan with a living room, dining area, and kitchen and has concrete flooring, splashes of wood, and glass walls. The mansion features a futuristic kitchen with a spacious island, custom cabinetry, and cutting-edge appliances. Other features of the fabulous beach house include a cozy nook, a sea-view office space, a family room, a media room, and an underground lounge room, making it a model home.

Aaron Rodgers’ shelled out $28 Million for this exquisite Malibu mansion, and it is pretty evident that it was worth every penny. The stunning beaches offer a calming getaway from the city life's hustle and bustle, providing ample leisure opportunities. The mansion has perfectly exploited the advantages of its highly sought-after location and is designed to deliver maximum luxury and comfort for Aaron Rodgers and his guests.
