Though Kristen Stewart became famous for her portrayal of Bella Swan in the Twilight Saga, she had done different movies that also did well at the box office. Stewart, who began acting at the early age of eight, has portrayed roles in numerous movies other than The Twilight Saga. Before she even starred in the vampire-themed movie, she had already done a lot of small roles in different movies.

Kristen Stewart was born on April 9, 1990, to parents John Stewart and Jules Stewart, who both worked in the film and television industry. This inspired her to enter the show business as well but as an actress. Following her Twilight Saga success, she had done different movies that made box office as well. Featured in this article are the movies that Kristen Stewart lead into the box office.

Café Society

The movie was set in the 1930s which features a Bronx native who moved to Hollywood and fell in love with a young woman who has a relationship with a married man. The romantic comedy movie stars Kristen Stewart and Jesse Eisenberg as leads. The rest of the cast includes Parker Posey, Corey Stoll, Blake Lively, Steve Carell, and Jeannie Berlin. The movie did well at the box office with $44 million gross sales.

Still Alice

Earning $44 million in the box office, the movie Still Alice lands the tenth spot with Café Society. The movie, led by Julianne Moore as Dr. Alice Howard, revolves around a renowned linguistics professor working at Columbia University. When the professor's Alzheimer's disease began to show symptoms, she struggled to continue her daily activities. The movie features Julianne Moore, Alec Baldwin, Kate Bosworth, and Kristen Stewart.

The Messengers

With $55 million earnings on the box office, the movie The Messengers lands the eighth spot. The movie is about a family who used to live in outrageous big city life and relocated to a simple and quiet North Dakota farm. Kristen Stewart, who played the teenage Jess, started seeing weird apparitions with her younger brother. Both of them endured the attacks from an unexplained source. She made her mission to give warning to her family before it was too late to even save them. The movie features Penelope Ann Miller, Dylan McDermott, John Corbett, and Kristen Stewart.

Into The Wild

With $56 million earnings on the box office, the movie Into The Wildlands is the seventh spot on the list. The movie is about a top graduating student from Emory University named Christopher McCandless. McCandless decided to abandon all his possessions and gave his entire savings of $24,000 to charity to go hitchhiking to Alaska. He abandoned his promising future to live in the wilderness. The story shows the daily encounters of the lead character that helped him shape his life. The movie features Marcia Gay Harden, Vince Vaughn, Catherine Keener, and Emile Hirsch.

Charlie’s Angels

Earning $58 million in the box office, the movie Charlie's Angels lands the sixth spot on the list. The comedy-action film is a remake of the Charlie's Angels series, which was released beginning in the year 2000. The movie was written and directed by the prominent actress Elizabeth Banks which was inspired by the story written by David Auburn and Evan Spiliotopoulos. The movie features Naomi Scott, Ella Balinska, and Elizabeth Banks.

The Flintstones in Viva Rock Vegas

Earning $59 million in the box office, the movie The Flintstones in Viva Rock Vegas lands the fifth spot on the list. The story is about Fred Flintstones, who courts the beautiful and young heiress Wilma Slaghoople. Flintstones had lived on the wrong side of the rocks, decided to go to Rock Vegas with best friend Barney Rubble and his fiancée Betty, Fred, and Wilma head for a romantic weekend getaway in the Rock Vegas. The movie features Stephen Baldwin, Kristen Steward, Mark Addy, and Jane Krakowski.

Zathura: A Space Adventure

With the movie earning $64 million in the box office, Zathura: A Space Adventure takes the fourth spot. The movie is about two young boys who were left in the care of their teen sister Lisa, and they all must stay inside the house. Thinking the day would be just another boring day, the two boys decided to play the space-themed board game, Zathura. They later realized that the board game had a magical power when their house was suddenly shot into space. The movie also features Tim Robbins, Josh Hutcherson, Jonah Bobo, and Dax Shepard.

Panic Room

With the movie earning $196 million in the box office, Panic Room takes the third spot on the list. The story is about a divorced woman who has a diabetic daughter; the two of them take refuge in the safe room of their newly purchased house when three men break into their house. The three men were searching for the missing fortune. The movie also features Jodie Foster, Forest Whitaker, and Dwight Yoakam.

Jumper

Earning $222 million in the box office, the movie Jumper takes the second spot. The movie is about a teenager who is capable of teleportation found himself in the torn middle of an ancient war between the beings like him that has powers and their sworn annihilators. The movie also features actors Hayden Christensen, Jamie Bell, Samuel L. Jackson, and Rachel Bilson.

Snow White And The Huntsman

With whopping earnings of $397 million at the box office, the movie Snow White and the Huntsman takes the top spot on the list. The movie is about a twisted fairy tale; the Huntsman was given orders to take Snow White in the middle of the woods and kill her there. As fate would have it, the Huntsman became Snow White's protector and mentor instead of being her killer. The two of them conspired to vanquish the reign of the evil queen. The movie also features Chris Hemsworth, Sam Claflin, and Charlize Theron.
