    Elizabeth Ries Twin Cities Live

    After her internship, Elizabeth began her career in Green Bay, Wisconsin, as a reporter and anchor for WBAY. She then moved to Duluth, Minnesota, where she did the morning Newscast for KDLH, finally moving back to the Twin Cities where she reported for 5 Eyewitness News.

    In 2009, Ries joined the Twin Cities Live team. She has had two different co-hosts until Steve Patterson was signed in July 2014. There was a stage where the show could have been canceled but ended up being expanded to 90 minutes instead.
