Early Life and Bio

Steven John Wilkos was born in March of 1964 in Chicago, Illinois to parents Stanley and Jeanette (née Pelikan). His father was a police officer who served in the Korean War, while his mother worked as a beauty school instructor.

Meanwhile, Wilkos has two sisters and one brother who all grew up in what is now the Roscoe Village neighborhood of North Center, Chicago.

As far as education goes, Wilkos graduated from Lane Technical High School in 1982. Then, he joined the United States Marine Corps and remained in for seven years, serving in countries like South Korea and Japan.

Afterward, Wilkos joined the Chicago Police Department and was stationed in his home city.

Steve Wilkos Net Worth and Career

In 1994, Wilkos took a part-time job as a security guard on Jerry Springer. Eventually, he began working there full-time and stayed in the position for over five years.

Later, Springer recruited Wilkos to substitute for him each Monday going forward. During this time, Wilkos appeared in episodes of Check, Please!, Between Brothers, and The Wayans Bros.

Lastly, The Steve Wilkos Show is now syndicated and airs on networks like Fox, MyNetworkTV, and The CW.
