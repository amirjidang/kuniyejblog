Michael Actress

Michael’s first role in either film or television was as Olivia Walton on The Waltons, which ran for nine seasons from 1972 to 1981. Since then she has starred in several films and television series. For instance, she starred as Nurse Mary Benjamin in the hospital drama Nurse, which ran on CBS for six episodes in spring 1981 and then for the 1981–1982 seasons. She also had starring roles in the unsuccessful 1988 drama Hothouse and 1989 sitcom Living Dolls and reprised her Waltons role for several television movies and reunions in the 1990s.

Michael played Judge Helen Turner in 2005 on the ABC soap operas All My Children and One Life to Live as part of the “baby switch” storyline on both shows. She portrayed Shirley Smith on ABC’s General Hospital. In 2011, she portrayed Katherine Chancellor on the CBS daytime soap opera, The Young and the Restless,
