Ian Ziering will forever be known as the ditzy yet warmhearted Steve Sanders on the teen series Beverly Hills, 90210. Did Ian Ziering Spend A Lot Of Money On His Divorce From Nikki Ziering?

Ian Ziering and Nikki Ziering were married from 1997 to 2002. According to the New Haven Register, Ian Ziering is the one who made the divorce filing, and he said that it was because of "irreconcilable differences." The publication reported that Nikki was a Playboy Playmate.

Although Ian became a big star thanks to Beverly Hills, 90210, not many publications seemed to report on his divorce. It's unknown if Ian Ziering had to pay Nikki spousal support or if the divorce cost him a lot of money.

However, since Ian Ziering has a net worth of $2 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth, it's possible that he did pay spousal support. At the very least, he would have had to pay a lawyer while he and Nikki worked out the details of their split.
