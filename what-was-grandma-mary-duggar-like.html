Mrs. Duggar Was Once A Sunday School Teacher

Mary Duggar taught Sunday school in Arkansas

In the 1990s, Mary Duggar was well-known in her small Arkansas community because she taught Sunday school at the First Baptist Church of Springdale. According to a Redditor who was a student of Mary Duggar's, their Sunday school days were wonderful because of Mrs. Duggar.

The self-described "bus kid," who said they were perceived as being lower-class than the local kids who attended the church, only had positive things to say about Mrs. Duggar.

"Mrs. Duggar was a wonderful woman who I greatly admired. She dressed beautifully, with high heels ðŸ‘  and her makeup and hair were always perfect. I didn't have any people in my home life that dressed so nicely so being exposed to her at a young age taught me that I wanted better for myself."

According to the insider, Mrs. Duggar always had time for her young charges and sometimes drove the children home as needed. Although they acknowledged that Mrs. Duggar likely taught hundreds of kids, the Redditor said that Mrs. Duggar was a kind woman and was dedicated to her job.

Another point in Mrs. Duggar's favor was that despite disagreeing with her son's choices, she continued to publicly support him.

Mrs. Mary Duggar Reportedly Disagreed With Jim Bob's Decisions

Mary Duggar was not thrilled about her son's large family

The Sunday school student of Mary Duggar's not only knew her during church hours, but was also introduced to the more famous Duggars sometime in the '90s. At that time, there were only a handful of Duggars, and they weren't nearly as famous (or infamous) as they are today (their tell-all book came out in 2008). So the churchgoing student was surprised when one day, Mrs. Duggar needed to make a stop before driving them home, and they met a whole row of Duggar kids.

The insider noted that they were "creeped out" by the way the kids lined up, because it reminded them of a time when they saw children disciplined in an identical line. However, they did not see anything untoward while they were in the Duggars' home for that brief time.
