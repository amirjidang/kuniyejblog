Blake Webber Biography – Blake Webber Wiki

Blake Webber (Aristotle Georgeson) is a comedian and social media influencer best known for his voice-over comedy videos. He adds his own distinct voice to Instagram videos of celebrities such as Cardi B, Amber Rose, Kim Kardashian among others.

The Webber character, which is the creation of Los Angeles-based comedian Aristotle Georgeson, gained a significant social media following after he was featured on Vice.

Georgeson rose to social media prominence for the Blake Vapes character from 2015, in which dons a signature “Hi Haters” hat and talks about how his life revolves around blowing “dope-ass clouds.” His other projects include the YouTube series including “#Killinit, Blake Pranks and 30 for 30. He is also known as half of the comedic DJ duo, Double Dare Music.

Webber signed with Gersh. In addition to Gersh, he is repped by Sam Leigh at inArtists and Naader Banki at Manatt, Phelps & Phillips, LLP.
