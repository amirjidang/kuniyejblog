About

James Cameron is a 69 year old Canadian Film Director. Born James Francis Cameron on 16th August, 1954 in Kapuskasing, Ontario, Canada and educated at Sonora High School (dropped out) and Brea Olinda High School, he is famous for directing big-budget hits like Terminator 2: Judgment Day (1991), Titanic (1997), and Avatar (2009), all of which broke box office records and pushed the boundaries for special effects in the entertainment industry in a career that spans 1975–present and 1974–present and 1978–present.
