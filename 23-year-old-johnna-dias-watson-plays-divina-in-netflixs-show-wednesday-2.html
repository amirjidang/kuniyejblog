23-Year-Old Johnna Dias-Watson Plays Divina In Netflixs Show Wednesday

Johnna Dias-Watson is well-known for portraying the role of Divina in the Netflix series “Wednesday.” Johnna is 23 years old.

In 2017, at a young age, she made her acting debut in David Bond’s production of the RWCMD drama Hedda Gabler. She then began appearing in plays such as Tartuffe, Moon Licks, Dance Nation, Hamlet & Crave for the Other Room, and Richard Burton Theatre Company.

She played Bella in the short film “I Got You,” written and directed by Casey Likes in 2020. After that, in 2022, Watson accepted the part of Bridesmaid in the A.J. Edwards-directed film “First Love.”

The other cast members of the series include Daiane Kruger, Nikolai Tsnakov, Chris Galust, Jeffrey Donovan, and others.
