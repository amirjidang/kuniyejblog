NJPW Road To Destruction Results (9/11): SANADA, EVIL, And More

On September 11, New Japan Pro-Wrestling continued its Road to Destruction tour with the fourth night of action from Tatsunoko Arena in Ryugasaki, Ibaraki, Japan.

The results, courtesy of Fightful, are as follows:

  •  YOH defeats Yuto Nakashima
  • TenKoji (Hiroyoshi Tenzan & Satoshi Kojima) defeat Togi Makabe & Oskar Leube
  • Shota Umino, Yuji Nagata & Six Or Nine (Master Wato & Ryusuke Taguchi) def. Strong Style (Minoru Suzuki, Ren Narita & El Desperado) & Tiger Mask
  • Be-Bop Tag Team (Hiroshi Tanahashi & Toru Yano) defeat Tomoaki Honma & Jado
  • CHAOS (Kazuchika Okada, Tomohiro Ishii, Hirooki Goto & YOSHI-HASHI) defeat TMDK (Zack Sabre Jr., Shane Haste, Bad Dude Tito & Mikey Nicholls
  • United Empire (Will Ospreay, Jeff Cobb, Great-O-Khan, Callum Newman & HENARE) def. Los Ingobernables de Japon (Shingo Takagi, Tetsuya Naito, Yota Tsuji, BUSHI & Hiromu Takahashi)
  • House Of Torture (SHO, EVIL, Dick Togo & Yujiro Takahashi) defeat Just 5 Guys (SANADA, Taichi, DOUKI & Yoshinobu Kanemaru)

WrestleZone will have coverage of NJPW Road to Destruction as it continues.
