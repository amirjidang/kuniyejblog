Are Joy-Anna Forsyth And Carlin Bates Still BFFs After Duggar Drama?

When the Duggar family first appeared on reality TV, viewers got to know some of their friends, too. The Bates family became recurring cast members on 19 Kids and Counting, and since the Bates had nearly as many kids as the Duggars, many of the kids formed lasting friendships with those around their ages.

Joy-Anna Duggar bonded with Carlin Bates, and the two formed a friendship that viewers hoped would last. But after the Duggars fell from grace not only on the national stage but within their closer circles as well, fans might wonder whether Joy and Carlin are still friends today.

Are The Duggars And Bates Still Friends?

There was some question of whether the Bates and Duggar families were still friendly, given what Josh Duggar did to his siblings, when the scandal went down. Although patriarch Gil Bates may have subtly shaded Jim Bob Duggar and his family, the grown-up Duggar and Bates kids still get to pick their friends.

And it seems that Joy-Anna and Carlin continue to pick one another, despite not living near each other anymore.

Joy-Anna Duggar Became Friends With Carlin Bates Years Ago

Like all other friendships in the Duggar household, Joy-Anna's bond with Carlin Bates was clearly supported by Jim Bob Duggar in the family's early days on TLC. At one point, the Duggars traveled to the Bates' home to help them with renovations to accommodate their growing family.

Like her now-husband Austin Forsyth, Joy-Anna's friend Carlin spent a lot of time with the family. Despite being labeled a tomboy, who Austin never really looked at romantically until she was older (leading some to suggest she was his last choice), Joy and Carlin bonded.

As they grew older, the two remained involved in one another's lives; Joy-Anna had Carlin Bates serve as her bridesmaid in her 2017 wedding. Two years later, Joy was Carlin's bridesmaid as she married Evan Stewart. Despite the wedding taking place in Tennessee, Joy traveled there from Arkansas to support her friend.

Where Does Carlin Bates Live Now?

Carlin Bates grew up with her family in Tennessee, while Joy-Anna grew up in Arkansas. The families got together when either the Bates or Duggars took road trips, though it's likely they also visited at conferences and other events tied to either their lifestyle or religion.

In 2022, Carlin and her husband Evan purchased their first home in Tennessee, moving out of a rental owned by a family friend (Carlin's brother Trace and his wife Lydia were going to move into the vacated rental).
