Tyson Beckford Responds To Chris Brown Over Karrueche Selfie

This might be one of the more random “beefs” we’ve seen thus far in 2015. Yesterday (May 24), Chris Brown took to Instagram and Twitter to share his discontent for model Tyson Beckford posting a selfie with his ex-girlfriend, Karrueche Tran, while in Las Vegas for the holiday weekend. Posting a series of not-so-subtle threats, the singer also made it clear that he would be heading to the city, alluding to a possible altercation with Beckford.

It seems Breezy’s threats did not go unnoticed, as Beckford took to Twitter with a response to the singer (albeit the wrong account), inviting him to the city for “fun:”

@officialchrisbrown it’s all about fun in vegas. Come thru papa.

— Tyson Beckford (@TysonCBeckford) May 25, 2015

Beckford also posted a video on Instagram that the masses have interpreted as a more aggressive response to Brown. Hitting the gun range, the model kept his caption short and sweet.

“Just in case you forgot! #pewpew #nra #bang #2a#5.56 #ar15 #edc #.762,” he wrote.

A source for E! News reports that Beckford is not looking to get involved with Karrueche, and that he wants no problems with Brown.

“Tyson was just being playful but it seemed that Chris got jealous,” our insider shared with E! News. “He is not looking for issues with Chris at all.”
